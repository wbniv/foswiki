---+ !QuickMenu is Disabled!

---++ Why is the !QuickMenu disabled?

The QuickMenuSkin requires a browser with Javascript enabled. Without Javascript the menu will not load and the usual links will not be accessible. This makes navigating through the TWiki difficult.

---++ Solution

You have 2 choices:

   * Use a browser that has javascript enabled.
   * Use a different TWiki skin like:
     [[%SCRIPTURLPATH{"view"}%/%TWIKIWEB%/PatternSkin?skin=pattern][PatternSkin]]
     or [[%SCRIPTURLPATH{"view"}%/%TWIKIWEB%/ClassicSkin?skin=classic][ClassicSkin]]
 %BR% To activate a skin see: [[TWikiSkins#Activating_Skins][TWikiSkins]]

%T% TWikiSkinBrowser lists all the skins installed on your server.
