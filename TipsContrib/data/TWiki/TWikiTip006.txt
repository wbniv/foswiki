---+ Creating a Table of Contents

The [[%SYSTEMWEB%.Macros][Macros]] =%<nop>TOC%= will automatically create a table of contents for a topic based on the headers of the topic. To exclude a heading from a =%<nop>TOC%= add =!!= to the end of the syntax for a header, e.g. write =---+!!= for a =---+= heading.
