#VarWIKINAME
---+++ WIKINAME -- your Wiki username
   * The WikiName is the same as =%<nop>USERNAME%= if not defined in the %USERSWEB%.%WIKIUSERSTOPIC% topic
   * Syntax: =%<nop>WIKINAME%=
   * Expands to: =%WIKINAME%=
   * __%X% Note:__ When used in a template topic, this variable will be expanded when the template is used to create  new topic. See TWikiTemplates#TemplateTopicsVars for details.
