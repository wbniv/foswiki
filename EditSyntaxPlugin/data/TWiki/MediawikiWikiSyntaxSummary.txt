---+ Wikimedia Wiki Syntax Summary 

This topic provides online help for users who activated the Mediawiki syntax in their user homepage with a =EDITSYNTAX = Mediawiki= setting. Syntax conversion is provided by the EditSyntaxPlugin.

Keeping this help text short and simple is important since this page is aimed at new users.  It does include pop-up links for more detailed help.

---
%STARTINCLUDE%
*Formatting help:*
   * *bold* put word/phrase in three single quotes: ='''your phrase'''=
   * *bullet list* start line with one or more asterisks and a space: =** your text=
   * *headings* put text in 1 to 6 equal signs: =<nop>==Your Heading==<nop>=
   * *italic* put word/phrase in two single quotes: =''your words''=
   * *internal links* use topic name or =[<nop>[link|label]]= rule: =WebHome=, or =[<nop>[WebHome|home]]=
   * *external links* use URL or =[url label]= rule: =http://yahoo.com=, or =[http://yahoo.com/ link to Yahoo]=
   * *monospaced* use HTML: =&lt;tt&gt;your words&lt;/tt&gt;=
   * *paragraphs* separate with blank line
