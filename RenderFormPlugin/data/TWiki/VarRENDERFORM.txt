#VarRENDERFORM
---+++ RENDERFORM -- render TWikiForms data within a topic
   * The =%<nop>RENDERLIST%= variable is handled by the RenderFormPlugin
   * Syntax: =%<nop>RENDERFORM{ "ATWikiFormDefinition"}%=
   * Example:%BR%
     =%<nop>RENDERLIST{ "TWiki.HelpDeskForm" }%= %BR%
   * Related: TWikiForms
